About This Golf Tee Selector

This website was created with one simple goal: to help golfers pick the right set of tees quickly and confidently.

Most golfers either play from tees that are too long, or they spend time debating which box is “correct.” That slows down play, increases frustration, and makes scoring more difficult than it needs to be.

This tool uses three core inputs—your driver carry distance, age, and handicap—to give a balanced, ego-free recommendation for where you’ll enjoy the game the most.

The tee calculator and the accompanying guides on this site are designed to work for:

  • New golfers learning course setup
  • Recreational players who want a fair challenge
  • Seniors and juniors adjusting yardage
  • Competitive players trying to match course length with ability
